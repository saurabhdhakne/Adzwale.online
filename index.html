About Us

Adzwale is the first ever initiative to manage social media promotions. Our aim is to make it easier for companies to find advertiser for their product as well as to provide opportunities to the content - creators to work for big deals by promoting their products.

what we provide.

We Connect

Interface where Companies are able to make post about their latest product, search for content - creators, negotiate and make a deal

Search

Content - Creators can find the product to market according to the category of their interest and bid for the same with the best amount and grab the deal.

No Restriction

No restrictions on selecting the number of Content - creators for single product as the content - creators have to bid for the deal.

what we do ?

Our portal provides an user interface that is shared among trusted companies and trusted content - creators. We do not charge for membership. Once Signed - up on the portal, members are provided certain rights.

Our Mission is to provide the Marketers and the Content - Creators, a single platform where Marketers can find best advertisers for their products and the Content - Creators can find best and valuable content for their followers.

Our Vision

We aim to work so hard that we become World’s No. 1 Portal such that every Marketer would want us to publish their product for advertisement and every Content – Creator would think of us to add value to his/ her content.

We aim at providing Manufacturers and Developers, the best platform to find their advertizers, and make their product popular and trendy via most followed talents.

We aim at providing Content - Creators, a platform to crack profitable deals with big brands simply by promoting their product in the content.
